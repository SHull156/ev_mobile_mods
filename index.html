About

My name is Robert Perry, and I’ve spent the last 20 years working in the automotive and engineering industry, building a career around high-quality workmanship, problem-solving, and a genuine passion for vehicles — especially electric ones.

Over the years, I’ve worked in a wide range of automotive and engineering workshops. In that time, I’ve led and delivered complex projects including full restorations, resto-mods, and electric vehicle conversions. My hands-on experience covers everything from classic cars like Facel Vegas and Land Rover Series 1s to larger, more unusual builds such as double-decker buses.

As electric vehicles have become a bigger part of the industry, I’ve specialised in EV servicing, electrical diagnostics, upgrades, and customisations, combining traditional mechanical knowledge with modern high-voltage systems. Safety and best practice are at the heart of everything I do — especially when working with EVs.

Alongside my workshop experience, I’ve spent several years tutoring and mentoring students, delivering training in welding, home vehicle maintenance, and safe electric vehicle working practices. I hold Hybrid and Electric Vehicle accreditation from The Institute of the Motor Industry, and I’ve helped both enthusiasts and professionals develop the skills and confidence to work safely and effectively.

Today, as a mobile mechanic, I bring that experience directly to you — whether you need routine servicing, fault finding, or bespoke EV modifications and custom work. I pride myself on clear communication, honest advice, and work done properly the first time. If you have a project in mind, no matter how small, do get in touch and I'll be happy to help.
