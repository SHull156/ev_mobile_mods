About

My name is Robert Perry. I have over 20 years’ experience in the automotive and engineering industry, built on high-quality workmanship, problem-solving, and a strong passion for vehicles — particularly electric ones.

I’ve worked across a wide range of workshops, delivering projects from full restorations and resto-mods to complex electric vehicle conversions. My experience spans classic cars such as Facel Vegas and Land Rover Series 1s, as well as larger and more unusual builds, including double-decker buses.

I now specialise in EV servicing, diagnostics, upgrades, and custom work, combining traditional mechanical expertise with modern high-voltage systems. Safety and best practice are central to everything I do.

I also hold Hybrid and Electric Vehicle accreditation and have several years’ experience training and mentoring others in welding, vehicle maintenance, and safe EV working practices.

Today, I operate as a mobile mechanic, travelling nationwide to bring this expertise directly to you — whether for routine servicing, fault-finding, or bespoke EV projects. I pride myself on clear communication, honest advice, and getting the job done right first time. If you have a project in mind, I’d be happy to help.
